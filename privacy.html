Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Bible Love ("we", "our", "us", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Bible Love mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • User-generated content: Prayer entries, devotion notes, journal entries, verse highlights, and chat messages with the AI companion.
  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, app version, and anonymous device identifiers.
  • Usage data: Features used, reading progress, and interaction patterns (anonymized).
  • Subscription information: Purchase history and subscription status (processed by Apple; we do not receive your payment details).

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or precise location unless you voluntarily provide them.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the App's features, including AI-generated devotions, prayer reminders, and Bible reading tools.
  • To personalize your spiritual experience (e.g., remembering your reading position).
  • To deliver prayer and devotion notifications you have enabled.
  • To analyze anonymized usage patterns and improve app quality.

3. Data Storage

Most of your personal data — including prayer entries, journal notes, and reading progress — is stored locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to our servers unless required for a specific feature (e.g., generating AI devotions).

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI / Anthropic: To generate AI devotions and companion chat responses. Your prompts may be sent to these services and are subject to their privacy policies.
  • Apple StoreKit: For subscription management and in-app purchases.
  • Apple Push Notification Service: For prayer reminders.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We only share data when required by law or when necessary to provide the App's core features (as described above).

6. Children's Privacy

Bible Love is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, delete, or export your locally stored data at any time via the App's settings.
  • Disable notifications through your device settings.
  • Uninstall the App, which will remove all locally stored data.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

10. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the Republic of Singapore.
